Philippines

The Philippines is an archipelagic country in Southeast Asia made up of more than 7,000 islands. It is located in the western Pacific Ocean and is known for its rich culture, diverse languages, and beautiful natural landscapes such as beaches, mountains, and tropical forests. The capital city is Manila, while one of its major economic centers is Davao City. The country has a mix of cultural influences from indigenous traditions as well as Spanish, American, and Asian history due to centuries of trade and colonization. Most people speak Filipino and English, and Christianity is the dominant religion. The Philippines is also known for the hospitality of its people, vibrant festivals, and popular foods like Adobo and Lechon. Its economy is supported by industries such as agriculture, services, remittances from overseas workers, and a growing technology sector.
